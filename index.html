NT2204: Advanced Distributed Computing Systems

Detailed Description

As computing ecosystems shift from isolated clusters to hyper-connected heterogeneous nodes, the demand for scalable, resilient, and intelligent systems has reached unprecedented heights. This course provides a comprehensive exploration of the theoretical and practical dimensions of Distributed Systems at a graduate level.

We balance classic distributed foundations—such as Dataflow execution models and complex scheduling via MapReduce paradigms—with contemporary innovations in Cloud-native architectures and Serverless computing. A core focus of the curriculum is Distributed Intelligence, addressing how AI is trained and orchestrated across edge-cloud continuums while maintaining rigorous Security and Privacy standards.
